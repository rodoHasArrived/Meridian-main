System notes02 / 02

Palette
Base is a desaturated navy #08101A. Surface stack: card #0D1722 → panel #101C2B → soft #162334 → raise #1B2A3C. A single signal primary #2AB2D4 carries all CTAs, active nav, and focus rings. Semantic tones are reserved: success, warning, danger, plus environment paper and live.
Typography
Three families, strict roles. Space Grotesk 600 for display titles, restrained and compact. IBM Plex Sans 400/500 for UI prose and labels. IBM Plex Mono — tabular — for every number, identifier, timestamp, and KPI. Headings and metrics use letter-spacing: -0.01em and sit tight.
Geometry
Radii step 4 / 6 / 8 / 10 px — no soft pillows. Default border #1F344C at full opacity; semantic borders sit at 30%. Shadows are 1–2 px drops with a faint inset highlight; no glow. Spacing snaps to a 4 px grid.
Data density
Tables: padding: 7px 12px, 12 px mono cells, right-aligned numerics with tabular figures. Tags are 10 px, 3 px radius. KPI cards are 22 px mono values — never display numerals. Spark and trend indicators use arrows (▲ ▼ ▬), never colored arrows alone.
Motion
200 ms ease. No bounce, no scale, no parallax. Focus is a 2 px cyan ring at 18% opacity. Async states use spinners on icons only, never on whole components.
